India — Must-Visit Tourist Places

A tiny showcase of iconic destinations across India. Click a card to learn more and plan your trip.

Taj Mahal — Agra

Uttar Pradesh · Mughal architecture · UNESCO icon

A timeless marble mausoleum set on the Yamuna’s banks — famous for sunrise views and intricate inlay work. Best visited early morning to avoid crowds.

Best time: Oct–Mar

Jaipur — The Pink City

Rajasthan · Forts & palaces · Culture & shopping

Amber Fort, Hawa Mahal and colourful bazaars define Jaipur’s royal charm. Great for heritage walks, handicrafts and Rajasthani cuisine.

Best time: Oct–Mar

Kerala Backwaters — Alleppey

Kerala · Houseboats · Tropical lagoons

Slow down on a traditional houseboat through palm-fringed canals — peaceful mornings, fresh seafood and lush landscapes make it a relaxing escape.

Best time: Sep–Mar

Goa — Beaches & Nightlife

Goa · Beaches · Food & parties

Sun, sand and seafood — Goa blends Portuguese history with laid-back beaches and lively nightlife. Ideal for both chill days and beach parties.

Best time: Nov–Feb

Varanasi — The Spiritual Heart

Uttar Pradesh · Ghats & river rituals · Pilgrimage

A deeply spiritual city on the Ganges where sunrise boat rides and evening aartis offer an unforgettable cultural experience.

Best time: Oct–Mar

Leh & Ladakh — High-Altitude Beauty

Jammu & Kashmir · Mountain passes · Rugged scenery

Crisp air, dramatic mountain valleys and remote monasteries — perfect for road trips, biking and stargazing in thin, clear skies.

Best time: May–Sep

Udaipur — City of Lakes

Rajasthan · Lakes & palaces · Romantic

Mirror-like lakes, ornate palaces and sunset boat rides create a romantic atmosphere — a favourite for couples and photographers.

Best time: Oct–Mar
